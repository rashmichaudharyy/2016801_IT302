The Internet

A network is a group of connected, communicating devices such as computers and printers. An internet is two or more networks that can communicate with each other. The most notable internet is called The Internet composed of hundreds of thousands of interconnected networks.

About

A global system of interconnected computers, using a standardised Internet Protocol suite for communication and sharing information is called the Internet. ISP stands for Internet Service Provider. This helps in providing direct access for using the internet from your office or home, connected through landlines. With the introduction of Wi-fi and broadband, connecting to the Internet has become wireless.
